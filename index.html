This comprehensive study explores the analytical potential and challenges of Laser Desorption/Ionization Fourier Transform Ion Cyclotron Resonance Mass Spectrometry for characterizing dissolved organic matter corona formation on nanoparticles.

DOM-Drivers

Comprehensive machine learning framework for predicting dissolved organic matter (DOM) composition across central European aquatic systems. This research project integrates high-resolution FT-ICR MS data with environmental variables to develop predictive models identifying key drivers of DOM composition. The framework implements multivariate random forest algorithms, correlation-based feature selection, and advanced statistical validation methods to achieve robust predictions of molecular formula patterns and environmental drivers.
